CodeStrom 2025

Get ready for the most competitive coding event of the year! CodeStrom brings together the brightest minds for an epic 6-hour coding battle on HackerRank platform. Test your problem-solving skills, compete with peers, and win exciting prizes!

Event Overview

  • Date: August 4, 2025
  • Time: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM (6 Hours)
  • Venue: Seminar Hall, ISBM College of Engineering
  • Platform: HackerRank
  • Capacity: Maximum 150 Participants
  • Certificates: Participation certificates for all

Event Schedule

09:30 AM - 10:00 AM
Registration & Check-in
Participants arrive and complete registration process
10:00 AM - 10:15 AM
Opening Ceremony
Welcome speech and event rules briefing
10:15 AM - 04:00 PM
Coding Competition
6-hour intensive coding session on HackerRank
04:00 PM - 04:30 PM
Results & Prize Distribution
Announcement of winners and prize distribution

Rules & Guidelines

  • Individual participation only
  • Laptops and internet connection will be provided
  • No external devices or internet allowed
  • Multiple programming languages supported
  • Real-time leaderboard updates
  • Plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification
  • Judges' decision will be final
  • All participants must follow college code of conduct
