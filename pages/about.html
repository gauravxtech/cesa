About CESA

Where Passion Meets Purpose

Who We Are

The Computer Engineering Students Association (CESA) is the official student-led body of the Computer Engineering Department at ISBM College of Engineering. Established with the aim of empowering students and enhancing their technical, professional, and personal growth, CESA serves as a vibrant platform for aspiring engineers to explore, innovate, and excel.

Our Vision

To foster a thriving ecosystem of innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning among students, while bridging the gap between academic knowledge and industry expectations.

Our Mission

  • To create a dynamic community of future-ready technologists and leaders.
  • To organize workshops, seminars, hackathons, and competitions.
  • To promote peer learning and real-world problem-solving.
  • To provide leadership and teamwork opportunities.
  • To build strong industry connections.
